================================================================================
SAMPLE DEMO QUERIES FOR SUPPORT RESOLUTION CLAW
================================================================================

Use these queries in the Streamlit UI to verify the three pipeline outcomes:
Resolved, Escalated (Medium Severity), and Escalated (High Severity).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OUTCOME A: RESOLVED (Confidence >= 3.0, Answers directly from FAQ Knowledge Base)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. "What documents do I need to register as an Eko merchant?"
   - Expected: Resolved. Returns onboarding checklist details.
   
2. "I am 17 years old, can I sign up as a micro-entrepreneur?"
   - Expected: Resolved. Returns info that age requirement is 18 years old.
   
3. "How do I reset my MPIN in the Eko partner app?"
   - Expected: Resolved. Returns instructions on clicking 'Forgot MPIN' and using OTP.
   
4. "My app says Server Unavailable and is loading endlessly. How do I fix it?"
   - Expected: Resolved. Returns troubleshooting steps for app network/cache issues.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OUTCOME B: ESCALATED - MEDIUM SEVERITY (Confidence < 3.0, KYC / Operational issues)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. "My uploaded PAN card was rejected but the spelling is correct. Help me verify."
   - Expected: Escalated (Medium). Suggests human agent check PAN verification response code.
   
6. "I did the video KYC 5 hours ago and my account is still pending. Can you approve?"
   - Expected: Escalated (Medium). Suggests manual document review in the admin panel.
   
7. "The app gave an error and my customer's Aadhaar cash withdrawal is pending for 2 days."
   - Expected: Escalated (Medium). Suggests human checking bank transaction logs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OUTCOME C: ESCALATED - HIGH SEVERITY (Confidence < 3.0, Financial loss / Compliance)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. "A customer is complaining that Rs. 10000 was debited but transaction failed. I need to register a chargeback dispute."
   - Expected: Escalated (High). Triggers wallet freeze hold and document proof collection.
   
9. "Help! A customer tricked me into sharing my wallet OTP and now Rs. 25000 is stolen from my Trade balance."
   - Expected: Escalated (High). Triggers temporary wallet freeze and fraud compliance alert.
   
10. "A police inspector visited my shop with a notice about an illegal money transfer. Where do I send the document?"
    - Expected: Escalated (High). Triggers immediate escalation to Eko Legal Desk.
