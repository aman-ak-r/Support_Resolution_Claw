   - Expected: Resolved. Returns that minimum age requirement is 18 years.

3. "How do I reset my MPIN in the Eko partner app?"
   - Expected: Resolved. Returns 'Forgot MPIN' OTP flow instructions.

4. "My app says Server Unavailable. How do I fix it?"

5. "How do I reactivate my CSP outlet after it was deactivated?"
   - Expected: Resolved (NEW - CSP category). Returns reactivation fee and re-verification steps.

6. "When will my settlement arrive? I did a transaction 2 days ago."
