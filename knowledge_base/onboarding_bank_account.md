# FAQ: Bank Account Requirements
    
Q: Can I use a joint bank account or savings account for Eko settlements?
A: Yes, you can use either a Savings Account or a Current Account. However:
1. The account holder's name MUST match the name on the PAN card submitted during onboarding.
2. Joint accounts are allowed ONLY if the applicant is the primary account holder.
3. Co-operative bank accounts are not recommended due to high settlement failure rates.

Please upload a clear picture of a cancelled cheque or bank passbook first page containing the account number, holder name, and IFSC code.
