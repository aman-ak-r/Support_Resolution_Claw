# FAQ: Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) on Commission
    
Q: Why is there a deduction on my earned commission?
A: As per Section 194H of the Income Tax Act, Eko is legally required to deduct 5% TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) on commission paid to partners.
- If you have not submitted a valid PAN card, TDS will be deducted at a rate of 20%.
- You can download your quarterly TDS certificates (Form 16A) from the Eko partner portal to claim tax credits.
