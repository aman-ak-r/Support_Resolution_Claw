# SOP: Physical Shop Verification
    
Physical verification of the merchant's business premises is required within 15 days of online registration:
1. An Eko distributor or authorized agent will visit your shop.
2. They will verify original PAN and Aadhaar documents.
3. They will capture a geo-tagged selfie with you in front of the shop.
4. If physical verification is not completed within 15 days, your transaction capabilities (especially cash-out and AePS) will be temporarily suspended.
