# SOP: Reactivation of Deactivated CSP Outlets

This SOP details how a deactivated CSP outlet can request reactivation.

## Requirements
1. Formal request submitted via the distributor portal.
2. Reactivation fee of Rs. 500.
3. Re-uploading of fresh shop photos with active GPS coordinates.
4. Complete biometric re-verification within 3 working days of request.

## Timeline
Reactivation processes take up to 48 hours after all required documents are validated.
