# SOP: Handling Chargebacks and Disputes
    
A chargeback occurs when a cardholder disputes a transaction made at your shop through their issuing bank.
1. When Eko receives a chargeback dispute, we will immediately freeze the disputed amount in your Trade Wallet.
2. We will request proof of service (copy of registration register, signature of customer, customer ID proof, and receipt).
3. You must provide this proof within 48 hours of notification.
4. If valid proof is provided, the chargeback is resolved, and the wallet freeze is lifted. Otherwise, the amount is refunded to the customer.
