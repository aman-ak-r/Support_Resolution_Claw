# SOP: KYC Document Rejection Criteria

This SOP lists the common reasons why merchant KYC documents are rejected.

## Rejection Reasons
1. **Name Mismatch**: Name on PAN card does not match the Aadhaar registry details.
2. **Blurry Images**: Uploaded images are unreadable or have poor resolution.
3. **Format**: Uploading documents in formats other than JPEG, PNG, or PDF.
4. **File Size**: Uploaded files exceeding 5MB.
