# FAQ: Transaction Limits for Eko Merchants
    
Q: What are the daily transaction limits for Eko services?
A: Limits are set by banks and NPCI regulations:
- **DMT (Domestic Money Transfer)**: Rs. 25,000 per sender mobile number per month.
- **AePS Cash Withdrawal**: Rs. 10,000 per customer Aadhaar per day. Max 5 transactions per customer per day.
- **mATM (Micro ATM)**: Rs. 10,000 per debit card per day.
- **Wallet Cash-In / Out**: Rs. 2,00,000 daily limit for fully-verified KYC merchants.
