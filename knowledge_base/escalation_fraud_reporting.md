# SOP: Reporting Fraud and Scams
    
If you suspect fraud, a customer scam, or unauthorized wallet transactions:
1. Immediately change your MPIN and log out of all active sessions.
2. Contact the Eko Emergency Helpline at +91-XX-XXXX-XXXX to request a temporary wallet freeze.
3. Send an email to fraud-alert@eko.co with transaction details, recipient details, and a brief description of the incident.
4. Eko compliance will review the case and cooperate with cyber crime authorities.
