# SOP: Legal Notices and Compliance Concerns
    
For any legal notices, police compliance demands, or official regulatory inquiries:
1. Do not share any details over chat or phone with unverified agents.
2. Direct all official legal correspondence to Eko's Legal Desk: legal.compliance@eko.co.
3. Any merchant violating compliance or participating in illegal cash conversions will be permanently blacklisted, and their KYC documents will be shared with the relevant authorities.
