# SOP: Commission Payout Frequencies and Schedules
    
Eko pays out commissions on a structured schedule:
1. **Real-time Commission**: Earned instantly on DMT, AePS, and Bill Payments. This is credited directly to your Eko Trade Wallet.
2. **Monthly Bonus / Slabs Adjustments**: Credited by the 5th working day of the subsequent month.
3. **Weekly Payouts**: Certain premium distributor commissions are settled every Tuesday.

If Tuesday is a bank holiday, weekly payouts are processed on the next working day.
