# FAQ: Eko Commission Slabs
    
Q: How much commission do I earn on transaction services?
A: Commission rates vary based on transaction volume and type:
- **Domestic Money Transfer (DMT)**: Earn up to 0.50% of the transaction value, capped at Rs. 15 per transaction.
- **Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AePS)**: Flat commission based on slab:
  - Rs. 100 - Rs. 500: Rs. 0.25 commission
  - Rs. 501 - Rs. 1000: Rs. 1.00 commission
  - Rs. 1001 - Rs. 2000: Rs. 3.00 commission
  - Rs. 2001 - Rs. 3000: Rs. 6.50 commission
  - Rs. 3001 - Rs. 10000: Rs. 9.00 commission
- **Micro ATM (mATM)**: Matches AePS slabs.
