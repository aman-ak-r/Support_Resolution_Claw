# SOP: Mobile Device Compatibility
    
The Eko Partner App is optimized for Android devices.
- **Minimum OS version**: Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher.
- **RAM**: Minimum 2GB (4GB recommended for faster fingerprint scanner integrations).
- **Storage**: At least 100MB free space.
- **Biometric Device**: Morpho, Mantra, or Startek scanner with updated RD services for AePS transactions.

The app is currently not supported on iOS (Apple devices) or Windows Phones. Please ensure Google Play Services is updated to the latest version.
