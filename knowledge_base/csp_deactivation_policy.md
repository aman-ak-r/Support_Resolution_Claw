# SOP: CSP Outlet Deactivation Policy

This document details the conditions under which a Customer Service Point (CSP) outlet may be deactivated.

## Deactivation Triggers
1. **Inactivity**: The CSP does not perform any financial transaction for 90 consecutive days.
2. **Compliance Violations**: Charging customers extra fees beyond prescribed slabs.
3. **Voluntary**: The merchant requests closure of their outlet.

## Effect
Once deactivated, all transaction portals, AePS withdrawals, and DMT transfers are locked immediately.
