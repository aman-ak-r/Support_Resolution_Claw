# SOP: Resolution for Device Blocked Error
    
If you see the error "Device Blocked" or "Device registration limit reached":
1. Eko permits a single partner to log in from only one device at a time for security.
2. If you switched phones or reinstalled the OS, your old registration token remains active.
3. To reset your device binding, go to Profile -> Reset Device in the web portal or contact your local distributor.
4. The system will send an OTP to your registered mobile number to confirm the device reset. Once validated, you can log in on the new device.
