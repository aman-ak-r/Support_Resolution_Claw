# SOP: Troubleshooting Rejected PAN Card
    
PAN card rejection usually happens due to:
1. **Name Mismatch**: The name on the PAN card must exactly match the name entered in the registration form and the Aadhaar details. Minor spelling variations will cause automated rejection.
2. **Blurry Upload**: The uploaded image must be clear, with the photo, PAN number, signature, and DOB fully legible.
3. **Invalid Card**: Blocked, cancelled, or inactive PAN cards will fail verification.
4. **Minor PAN**: PAN cards issued to minors (under 18) are rejected automatically.

To resolve: Upload a clean photo of the original PAN card (not a photocopy) and verify the spelling matches.
