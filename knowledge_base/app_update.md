# FAQ: App Update and Installation Troubleshooting
    
Q: I am unable to update my Eko app. What should I do?
A: Follow this troubleshooting checklist:
1. Open Google Play Store and search for "Eko Partner App".
2. If "Update" is not showing, uninstall the app and reinstall it.
3. Ensure your phone has at least 150MB of internal storage free.
4. Check if your phone's Android version is below 7.0. Older versions are no longer supported.
