# SOP: Customer Service Point (CSP) Registration

This document outlines the detailed requirements and process for registering a new Customer Service Point (CSP) outlet on the Eko network.

## Requirements
1. Active bank account in the merchant's own name.
2. Clean police verification certificate (PCC) issued within the last 6 months.
3. Proof of shop ownership or a valid rental agreement.
4. Security deposit of Rs. 1000 payable upon registration.

## Process
1. Merchant submits registration details via the distributor portal.
2. The back-office team reviews documents within 2 business days.
3. Upon approval, CSP credentials are sent to the registered mobile number.
