# FAQ: Age Limit for Eko Partners
    
Q: What is the age requirement to become an Eko partner?
A: You must be at least 18 years of age at the time of registration. Eko cannot register minors as merchants due to legal and financial compliance regulations in India. 

Verification is done using your Aadhaar or PAN card. If you are under 18, you cannot register. Do not attempt to use family member details as it violates Eko's terms of service and leads to permanent suspension.
