# SOP: Resolving Partial Debits

Instructions for merchants when a customer's bank account is debited but the Eko transaction status is FAILED.

## Reconciliation
1. Under NPCI regulations, partial debits are reversed by the customer's bank within 3 to 5 business days.
2. Eko does not hold partially debited funds.
3. Merchants must provide the customer with the failed receipt and advise them to contact their bank.
