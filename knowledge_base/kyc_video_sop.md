# SOP: Video KYC Verification Guidelines
    
To prevent identity fraud, Eko requires a short video KYC:
1. Start the video KYC recording inside the app.
2. Hold your original PAN card and Aadhaar card close to your face.
3. Look directly into the camera and read the 4-digit code displayed on the screen clearly in English or Hindi.
4. The video must be shot in a well-lit room, and no third-party should be visible or audible.
5. The process takes less than 30 seconds. Approval takes 2-4 hours.
