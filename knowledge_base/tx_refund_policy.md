# SOP: Refund Policy for Failed Transfers
    
1. For failed money transfers where Eko has charged the merchant's wallet, refunds are initiated automatically:
   - IMPS failures: Refunded to wallet within 10 minutes.
   - NEFT failures: Refunded to wallet within 24 hours.
2. If a transaction status remains pending, the refund will not be initiated until the bank gateway declares it failed (which takes up to 72 hours max).
3. Eko does not levy any charges on refunded transactions.
