# SOP: Customer Support Escalations
    
If a merchant's problem is not resolved through the standard FAQ or the Eko Partner App:
1. Provide the partner with the toll-free customer care number: 1800-XXX-XXXX.
2. Alternatively, direct them to email support@eko.co with their registered merchant code.
3. If there is an unresolved transaction issue pending for more than 7 days, escalate directly to the nodal officer at nodal.officer@eko.co.
