# FAQ: Password or PIN Reset on Eko App
    
Q: How do I reset my login password or transaction PIN (MPIN)?
A: To reset:
1. Click the "Forgot Password" or "Forgot MPIN" link on the login screen.
2. Enter your registered Eko mobile number.
3. You will receive an OTP. Enter it in the app.
4. Set a new password (must be alphanumeric, at least 8 characters) or 4-digit MPIN.

Never share your OTP, password, or MPIN with anyone, including Eko customer support agents.
